Michael Suen is an independent design lead based in Queens, New York. From authoring point-and-click adventures in the 1990s to building living brands and software experiences in the 2020s, he’s been carving out corners of the internet for nearly two decades. He now puts his motley skills to use as the founder of Weirdo, a go-to-market design studio helping uncommon companies become themselves.
